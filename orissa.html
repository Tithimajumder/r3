Odisha

245+ reviews

Odisha is a state located on the eastern coast of India, bordering the Bay of Bengal. It is known for its rich history, ancient temples, vibrant culture, and stunning natural landscapes.

Bhubaneswar, the capital city of Odisha, is often referred to as the "Temple City of India." It is home to numerous magnificent temples that showcase exquisite architecture and intricate craftsmanship. The Lingaraja Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is one of the most prominent temples in Bhubaneswar, featuring a unique blend of Kalinga and Nagara architectural styles. The Mukteshwar Temple, Rajarani Temple, and Ananta Vasudeva Temple are also notable landmarks in the city. Puri, another significant city in Odisha, is famous for the Jagannath Temple, one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites for Hindus. The temple is dedicated to Lord Jagannath (a form of Lord Vishnu) and hosts the world-famous Rath Yatra festival, during which the deities are ceremoniously pulled in grand chariots by devotees.

Odisha is a state that captivates with its ancient temples, natural wonders, vibrant festivals, and rich cultural heritage. Whether it's exploring the architectural marvels, soaking in the serenity of its beaches, or experiencing the warmth of its people, Odisha offers a memorable journey into the heart of Eastern India.
