Haryana

160+reviews

Haryana is a state located in northern India. It is bordered by Punjab and Himachal Pradesh to the north, Rajasthan to the west and south, and Uttar Pradesh to the east. Haryana is known for its agricultural significance, rich history, and cultural heritage.

Chandigarh serves as the shared capital of both Haryana and Punjab. It is a modern and well-planned city, designed by renowned architect Le Corbusier. Chandigarh is famous for its architecture, urban planning, and beautiful gardens, including the Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake, and Rose Garden.

Haryana has a strong agricultural base and is often referred to as the "Granary of India." The state is known for its production of wheat, rice, millet, and vegetables. The agricultural prosperity has contributed significantly to the state's economy.

The state offers a unique blend of rich history, cultural heritage, agricultural significance, and traditional arts. With its historical sites, natural beauty, and warm hospitality, Haryana provides a glimpse into the diverse cultural tapestry of northern India.
