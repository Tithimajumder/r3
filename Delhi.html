Delhi

245 reviews

Delhi is steeped in history, with numerous landmarks that reflect its ancient past. From the UNESCO World Heritage sites of Qutub Minar and Red Fort to the iconic Jama Masjid and Humayun's Tomb, the city is a treasure trove of architectural marvels that chronicle centuries of dynasties and empires.

Delhi serves as a major transportation hub, with well-connected international and domestic airports, railway stations, and an extensive metro network. It provides easy access to other parts of India and serves as a gateway to explore the country's diverse landscapes and heritage.

It is a food lover's paradise, renowned for its diverse and delectable cuisine. From street food delights like chaat, kebabs, and parathas to fine dining experiences in upscale restaurants, the city tantalizes taste buds with its flavorful dishes, representing various regional and international cuisines.
