Sikkim

175+ reviews

Sikkim is a state located in northeastern India, nestled amidst the majestic Himalayan mountains. Known for its stunning natural beauty, Sikkim is often referred to as the "Land of the Mystic" or the "Hidden Paradise." Despite being the second-smallest state in India, Sikkim is rich in diverse landscapes, including snow-capped mountains, lush valleys, glistening lakes, and dense forests.

Sikkim is renowned for its biodiversity and is recognized as a biodiversity hotspot. It is home to a wide variety of flora and fauna, including rare orchids, rhododendrons, and elusive animals like the red panda and the snow leopard. The state has made significant efforts in preserving its natural heritage by establishing various national parks and wildlife sanctuaries.

In essence, Sikkim is a mesmerizing blend of natural beauty, cultural diversity, and sustainable practices. A visit to this enchanting state is an experience that leaves a lasting impression on all who explore its hidden treasures.
