Manipur

180+ reviews

Manipur is a state located in northeastern India, nestled among the foothills of the eastern Himalayas. It is known for its scenic landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant traditions. Manipur, often referred to as the "Land of Jewels," offers a unique blend of natural beauty, ethnic diversity, and artistic expressions. Imphal is the capital city of Manipur and serves as its cultural and administrative hub. The city is surrounded by picturesque hills and valleys, offering breathtaking views.

The scenic beauty of Manipur is unparalleled. The Loktak Lake, located near Imphal, is the largest freshwater lake in northeastern India and is known for its floating phumdis (heterogeneous mass of vegetation). The lake is home to unique flora and fauna, including the endangered Manipur brow-antlered deer (Sangai), which is the state animal of Manipur.

It is a state that captivates with its natural beauty, cultural richness, and artistic expressions. With its scenic landscapes, traditional arts, vibrant festivals, and warm hospitality, Manipur offers a delightful experience for travelers seeking to explore the lesser-known treasures of northeastern India.
