Kerala

245 reviews

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is a stunning state located on the southwestern coast of India. It is renowned for its lush green landscapes, serene backwaters, pristine beaches, and rich cultural heritage.

Kerala's backwaters, comprising tranquil lakes, canals, and lagoons, are a highlight of the state. Cruising through these scenic waterways on traditional houseboats, known as "kettuvallams," offers a peaceful and picturesque experience, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in the beauty of nature.

Kerala is blessed with breathtaking hill stations that boast mist-covered mountains, tea and spice plantations, and cascading waterfalls. Destinations like Munnar, Wayanad, and Thekkady provide a refreshing escape from the bustling city life, offering opportunities for trekking, wildlife encounters, and rejuvenation amidst nature's splendor.
