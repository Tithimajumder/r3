Arunachal Pradesh

220+ reviews

Arunachal Pradesh is a state located in the northeastern part of India, sharing borders with Bhutan, Tibet (China), and Myanmar. It is known as "The Land of the Rising Sun" due to its easternmost location in India, which causes the sun to rise here before any other part of the country. Arunachal Pradesh is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, diverse tribal cultures, and majestic Himalayan landscapes.

The state is blessed with abundant natural resources and is home to dense forests, snow-capped mountains, cascading waterfalls, and meandering rivers. Arunachal Pradesh is often referred to as the "Orchid State of India" due to its incredible variety of orchid species. The region's pristine environment and rich biodiversity make it a paradise for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers.

The Tawang Monastery, located in the town of Tawang, is one of the most significant Buddhist monasteries in the world and a major pilgrimage site for Buddhists. The monastery's stunning architecture, serene ambiance, and panoramic views of the surrounding mountains attract visitors from far and wide.

It's worth mentioning that due to the sensitive border situation and the presence of restricted areas, access to certain parts of Arunachal Pradesh requires obtaining a permit from the Indian government. It's advisable for visitors to check the current travel regulations and requirements before planning a trip. In conclusion, Arunachal Pradesh captivates visitors with its awe-inspiring landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. Its pristine natural beauty, coupled with the fascinating tribal cultures, offers a unique and immersive experience for those seeking an off-the-beaten-path adventure in India's northeastern frontier.
