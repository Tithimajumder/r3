Chandigarh

202+ reviews

Chandigarh is a union territory and capital city of two Indian states, Punjab and Haryana. It is a planned city located in the northern part of India, serving as a symbol of modern urban planning and design. Designed by renowned architect Le Corbusier, Chandigarh is known for its well-organized layout, beautiful architecture, and green spaces. Chandigarh is divided into several sectors, each planned to have its own residential, commercial, and recreational areas. The city's architectural landmarks, such as the Capitol Complex, Rock Garden, and Sukhna Lake, attract visitors from around the world.

The Capitol Complex, designed by Le Corbusier, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and houses the administrative buildings of the Punjab and Haryana governments. It includes the iconic Open Hand Monument, which is a symbol of peace and unity.

Chandigarh is known for its urban planning, with wide, tree-lined avenues, well-maintained parks, and green spaces. The Rose Garden, also known as Zakir Hussain Rose Garden, is the largest rose garden in Asia, showcasing thousands of varieties of roses. Other parks like the Leisure Valley and the Garden of Fragrance provide peaceful retreats for residents and visitors.It stands as a shining example of modern urban planning, blending architectural excellence, green spaces, and a high quality of life. Its well-designed layout, iconic landmarks, and cultural vibrancy make it a city worth exploring for its unique charm and distinctive character.
