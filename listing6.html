Places To Explore

Mizoram

Mizoram is a state located in the northeastern part of India. It is bordered by Myanmar (Burma) to the east and south, and by the Indian states of Assam, Manipur, and Tripura to the north and west. Known as the "Land of the Hill People," Mizoram is characterized by its scenic landscapes, vibrant tribal culture, and rich biodiversity.

Bihar

Bihar is a state located in the eastern part of India. It is one of the oldest inhabited regions in the world and has a rich historical and cultural heritage. Bihar is known for its ancient civilization, religious sites, educational institutions, and vibrant festivals.

Read More

Chattisgarh

Chhattisgarh is a state located in central India. It was carved out of Madhya Pradesh in the year 2000 and became the 26th state of India. Known as the "Rice Bowl of India" due to its abundant rice production, Chhattisgarh is a land of rich cultural heritage, tribal communities, ancient temples, and natural beauty.

Read More

Haryana

Haryana is a state located in northern India. It is bordered by Punjab and Himachal Pradesh to the north, Rajasthan to the west and south, and Uttar Pradesh to the east. Haryana is known for its agricultural significance, rich history, and cultural heritage.

Read More

Jharkhand

Jharkhand is a state located in eastern India. It was carved out of the southern part of Bihar in the year 2000 and became the 28th state of India. Jharkhand is known for its rich mineral resources, tribal culture, dense forests, and vibrant folk traditions.

Read More

Nagaland

Nagaland is a state located in the northeastern part of India. It is known for its rich tribal heritage, scenic landscapes, vibrant festivals, and warm hospitality. Nagaland is home to several indigenous tribal communities, with each tribe having its distinct language, customs, and traditions.
