Gujarat

220+ reviews

Gujarat, located in western India, is a state known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and thriving industries. With Gandhinagar as its capital city, Gujarat is the fifth largest state in terms of area and the ninth most populous state in India.

Gujarat has a remarkable historical significance as it was the birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi, the iconic leader of India's freedom struggle. The state takes immense pride in preserving Gandhian heritage, and the Sabarmati Ashram in Ahmedabad serves as a prominent reminder of his legacy.

Overall, Gujarat's rich cultural heritage, warm hospitality, and impressive industrial development make it a fascinating state to explore, attracting tourists, business travelers, and history enthusiasts alike.
