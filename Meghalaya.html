Meghalaya

245 reviews

Meghalaya is often referred to as the "Scotland of the East" due to its breathtaking natural beauty. The state is blessed with rolling hills, mist-covered valleys, and dense forests that house an incredible variety of flora and fauna. The stunning landscapes of places like Cherrapunji and Mawlynnong are a treat for nature lovers.

Meghalaya is famous for its unique living root bridges, which are natural wonders crafted by the indigenous Khasi and Jaintia tribes. These bridges are made by training the roots of rubber fig trees to form sturdy crossings over rivers and streams. The Double Decker Living Root Bridge in Cherrapunji is particularly awe-inspiring.

The state is dotted with numerous waterfalls that add to its ethereal beauty. The majestic Nohkalikai Falls, one of the tallest plunge waterfalls in India, and the mesmerizing Elephant Falls near Shillong are must-visit attractions. The region's abundant rainfall ensures that the waterfalls flow in their full glory.
