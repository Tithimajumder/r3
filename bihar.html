Bihar

260+ reviews

Bihar is a state located in the eastern part of India. It is one of the oldest inhabited regions in the world and has a rich historical and cultural heritage. Bihar is known for its ancient civilization, religious sites, educational institutions, and vibrant festivals.

Patna, the capital city of Bihar, is an important historical and cultural center. It was once the capital of the ancient kingdom of Magadha and has witnessed the rise and fall of several dynasties. Patna is home to many historical landmarks, including the iconic Golghar, Patna Museum, and the ancient ruins of Pataliputra.

Bihar has been a significant center of learning for centuries. Nalanda, located in the Nalanda district of Bihar, was once a renowned ancient center of learning and Buddhism. The ruins of Nalanda University are a UNESCO World Heritage Site and attract scholars and tourists from around the world.

Bodh Gaya, another prominent destination in Bihar, is a sacred place for Buddhists. It is where Gautama Buddha attained enlightenment under the Bodhi Tree. The Mahabodhi Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a major pilgrimage site for Buddhists and a place of tranquility and spiritual significance.

From its ancient civilizations and religious sites to its festivals, arts, and cuisine, Bihar offers a diverse and fascinating experience for travelers seeking to explore the heart of eastern India.
